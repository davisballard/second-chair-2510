# Second Chair — B2C Consumer Brand Name

**Brand Name:** Fair Case  
**Domain:** getafaircase.com  
**Decision Date:** March 12, 2026  
**Status:** Final

---

## The Decision

After three naming rounds (Sessions 15.01–15.03), **Fair Case** is the confirmed B2C consumer brand for Second Chair's personal injury lead generation product.

Previous working name **Claim Justice Now** is superseded. All ClaimJusticeNow identity work is archived at `01_Identity/ClaimJusticeNow/`.

---

## Why Fair Case Won

**"Fair" is the claimant's own word.** Consumer research across both naming rounds confirmed that accident victims don't use "justice" or "claim" in natural conversation. They say "I just want things to be fair." The word requires zero decoding — it's already in her vocabulary before she sees the ad.

**"Case" is accessible lay language.** "Claim" is what adjusters say. "Case" is everyday language — "you might have a case," "what's my case worth?" It carries lower litigation signal and is not yet owned in the B2C lead space the way "Claim" is.

**Secondary meaning.** Fair Case is not just a fair case *evaluation* — it's a case worth making. There's a built-in argument for fairness in the name itself.

**Domain:** getafaircase.com over faircase.net — .com authority wins for a consumer trust brand. The "get a" prefix functions as a CTA embedded in the URL. In all copy and creative, the brand is referred to as **Fair Case**. The URL is the action wrapper.
