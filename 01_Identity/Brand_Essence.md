## What We Are

Second Chair is a **PI lead generation company** built on a single conviction: the leads PI attorneys buy should be as honestly won as the cases they take.

We build advertising that honestly persuades claimants — producing leads who arrive with accurate expectations and the readiness to engage — because the advertising earned their response rather than manufacturing it.

### Practice Areas

**Personal Injury (PI/MVA)** — The full case spectrum:

- **Soft tissue / minor injury cases** — Whiplash, neck/back, minor fractures, general MVA with documented injuries. Consistent volume that sustains a practice.
- **High-value cases** — Commercial truck/18-wheeler, rideshare/Uber/Lyft (stacked insurance layers), serious spinal/back injuries, pedestrian/cyclist incidents, wrongful death.

### The Business Model

Second Chair's model is aligned to a single metric: **Cost-Per-Signed-Case (CPSC)**.

- Second Chair runs advertising on Meta (95–100% of B2C spend) under Special Ad Categories restrictions
- Consumer brand: **Fair Case**
- Leads are generated exclusively for one attorney partner per market — no shared leads
- Reporting leads with CPSC, not CPL, as the primary account health metric
- Month-to-month terms — no long-term contracts required
- Attorneys see the advertising before they commit

**What CPSC alignment means:** CPL tells an attorney what they paid to talk to someone. CPSC tells them what they paid to sign a case. These numbers are different in ways that matter. Second Chair always leads with CPSC.

## Mission

Every point in the PI advertising supply chain is honest — the creative is honest, the compliance is architectural, the reporting is transparent, and the claimant who responds was genuinely persuaded, not manipulated.

---

## Single-Minded Proposition

*"The leads PI attorneys buy should be as honestly won as the cases they take."*

**What "honestly won" means:**
A case is honestly won through genuine advocacy — the attorney believed in it, worked it, earned the outcome. A lead is honestly won through advertising that earns the claimant's response: specific, accurate information about what they have, emotional resonance that is true to their real situation, no manufactured urgency, no false claims about outcome. The claimant responded because something genuine moved them.

## Brand Character

Four traits. Each defined by what it IS and what it IS NOT.

| Trait | Is | Is Not |
|-------|-----|--------|
| **Expert, Not Arrogant** | Confident in the craft because the craft is real. Demonstrates something specific competitors can't match. | Chest-thumping. "Industry-leading," "best-in-class," or "cutting-edge." |
| **Honest in the Specific** | Uses exact numbers, real situations, specific processes. When uncertain, says so. | Values performance. Does not say "we're transparent" — is transparent. |
| **Respectful of Intelligence** | Treats the attorney as a capable evaluator. Speaks the language of case acquisition economics, not marketing. | Selling. No urgency tactics, competitor fear, or scarcity mechanics. |
| **Weighted, Not Heavy** | Serious in register without being stiff. Carries weight because the work has weight. | Corporate. Not jargon, not "disruption," not startup energy. A vendor that speaks like a principal. |

## Brand Promise

**To attorneys:** We build advertising you can evaluate before you commit — because we built it to be worth showing. Claimants who were honestly persuaded arrive with accurate expectations and convert at a higher rate. The ethical choice and the commercial choice are the same.

**To claimants (via Fair Case):** Before you respond to anything — here's what you should know. One specific fact. One minimum-barrier action. Information that respects your intelligence and your right to make your own decision.

## How Second Chair Is Different

The primary proof mechanism: **Second Chair shows the advertising it runs to generate leads before any attorney commits to a partnership.** No other PI lead gen vendor does this.

1. **Honest advertising** — Built in-house by a team with 18+ years of brand strategy and creative direction. Shown before commitment. Designed to earn the claimant's response, not manufacture it.
2. **CPSC as the primary metric** — Reported by default on every account. No other PI lead gen vendor does this without being asked.
3. **Compliance architecture** — TrustedForm full stack on every lead. Fail-closed DNC/litigator screening. No shared leads.
4. **Founding team integration** — Creative, compliance infrastructure, and media execution run by the same founding team. No vendor handoff where quality collapses.
5. **Month-to-month terms** — The business model assumes Second Chair must earn the relationship continuously.

---

## The Non-Negotiables

1. Never runs misleading advertising
2. Never shares leads between attorneys
3. Never hides the advertising from attorney partners
4. Never reports CPL as the primary metric
5. Never takes an attorney partner whose intake practices contradict what the advertising promises
6. Never pitches volume as the primary value
7. Never uses fear, artificial urgency, or manufactured scarcity in B2C advertising
8. Never claims to be something it isn't — yet

## What We Are NOT

- We are NOT a law firm
- We are NOT providing legal advice
- We are NOT recommending specific attorneys
- We DO NOT use "you" language for injuries/conditions in advertising
- We are NOT a tool company — we are an advertising company with compliance-grade infrastructure
