## Key Insights & Strategic Priorities

### The Math

1. **$90–$115 per founder per lead (Year 1 high-MOI markets).** That's the atomic unit. Drops to ~$83–$92 at full scale as lower-MOI markets fill in.
2. **One high-MOI Market at 3 leads/day = $97K–$124K/year per founder.** One client, one market. (At blended avg: $90K–$117K.)
3. **11 Markets (5% fill) crosses $1M/year per founder.** That's the first milestone. MOI-weighted pricing makes this slightly easier because early Markets are the highest-priced.
4. **Even at 1% close on outreach, we add 3 Markets/month.** Compounding with zero churn assumption.

### Strategic Priorities

1. **Dial in cost per lead** — Start at $150 target, optimize toward $75. Every dollar saved on CPL flows directly to the bottom line across every Market.
2. **Prove the conversion** — First 3–5 Markets establish the lead quality and attorney satisfaction that drives retention and referrals.
3. **Scale outreach systematically** — 300 reachouts/month is the baseline. Volume and close rate are the two levers that control growth speed.
4. **Build for retention** — Partner churn resets the compounding. Lead quality and attorney experience are the retention engine.
5. **Optimize lead volume per Market** — Ramp from 1–2 leads/day to 3–4 as creative and funnels mature in each market.
6. **Launch premium nurturing (Month 4–6)** — Build the AI call/chat/text infrastructure. Start with highest-trust partners. The 4–5x revenue multiplier per Market is the single biggest margin lever in the business.
7. **Introduce agency services (Month 4–6)** — Upsell brand identity, ad management, chatbots, email flows, websites. Deepens partner relationships and creates switching costs.
8. **Drive premium adoption** — Target 25% premium by end of Year 1, 40–60% by Year 2. Premium is where the business model transforms from lead gen into an intake infrastructure company.

### Cost Optimization Path

| Phase | Cost/Lead | Profit/Lead (at $420 Y1 avg) | Profit/Lead (at $350 scale avg) | Per Founder/Lead |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Launch (target)** | $150 | $270 | $200 | $90 / $67 |
| **Optimized** | $100 | $320 | $250 | $107 / $83 |
| **Fully optimized** | $75 | $345 | $275 | $115 / $92 |

Every $25 reduction in cost per lead at 100 Markets × 3 leads/day = **$2.25M additional annual profit** ($750K per founder).

### MOI Pricing Impact Summary

The shift from flat $400/lead to MOI-based pricing has three effects:

1. **Year 1 revenue is slightly higher** — launching in high-MOI markets means weighted avg is ~$420, not $400.
2. **Full-scale revenue is lower** — all 210 DMAs average ~$350, not $400. At 100% fill, per-founder annual run rate is ~$15M–$21M (vs. ~$19M–$25M under flat pricing).
3. **The tradeoff is defensibility** — every price can be explained with a methodology, not negotiated on a call. This builds the trust that drives retention, which is the real revenue lever.
