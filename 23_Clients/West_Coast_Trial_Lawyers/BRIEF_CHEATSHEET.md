## 10 Golden Rules

1. **Slow down.** Late-night DJ voice. Calm = control.
2. **Ask, don't tell.** 70% questions / 30% statements.
3. **Critique the industry, never his vendors.**
4. **Give him the insight, not yourself.** Every realization feels like his.
5. **Silence 4+s after every question.** Count internally. Don't rescue.
6. **Accusation audit at every transition.** Pre-empt what he's thinking.
7. **Arm him for leadership at every objection.** The real buyer isn't in the room.
8. **Precise numbers.** $5K-$100K, not "five figures." $360, not "around $350."
9. **Fairness trigger for pricing.** *"I want this to feel fair to both sides."*
10. **Don't compete on risk.** Refuse guarantees with questions, not counters.
