**Q1 — Neutral present-tense question about his current setup:**

**If Robert brings up a specific current vendor by name:**
> *"I'm not here to talk about any specific vendor. Every vendor in this space has a hard time with the same problem — compliance, visibility, attribution. What matters is whether how we're structured solves a problem you're actually carrying. Everything else is noise."*

**If Robert pushes hard for a guarantee:**
Deploy Guarantee Sequences 1, 2, 4, 12. End with: *"If I told you right now I could guarantee $3K CPSC on your first 100 LA leads — knowing everything we just talked about — would you actually trust that guarantee, or would it make you more suspicious of me?"*
