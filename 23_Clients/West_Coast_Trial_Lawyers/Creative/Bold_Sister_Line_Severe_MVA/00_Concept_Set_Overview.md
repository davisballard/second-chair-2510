## Why This Set Exists

The Cinematic Documentary line is the *credible* register for the Severe MVA volume slot — kitchen counter, walker on the LEFT, calloused right hand on a stack of Banner Gateway envelopes, restrained Mindhunter+Villeneuve+Coens grade. It does the campaign's substantiation work and clears Saul's gate cleanly. **It does not stop a thumb in a feed already saturated with Toyota Tacoma ads, Ridge Wallet, Mike Rowe Foundation, and three other PI billboard mills working in similar grammar.**

This sister-line cuts. Each finalist below lives in a register Jason *already consumes* (per `Jason/01_Life_World.md` §7) — and bends the wrapper to land the same SMP through different frequency. The principle: *a register-bend so culturally precise to the audience it reads as recognition, not advertising.*

**SMP recap (from `Jason/00` §4):** Jason's identity is built on a body that works and a crew that listens. A non-soft-tissue MVA hasn't just injured him — it has removed him from the role he built every other part of his life around. WCTL's job is to be the one specialist who treats him like he's still the competent man in his own room. Each concept honors that without ever naming it.

## Hard Guardrails (Saul + Massimo + Julian gates, applied per concept)

These are non-negotiable across every concept. Per-concept compliance flags inside each file extend these.

- **No infringement.** Aesthetic *register* only. PS2-era polygonal sports-cutscene aesthetic — never "Madden" or "NFL." Mike Rowe register — never *Dirty Jobs* assets or Mike Rowe's voice. KMLE country-radio register — radio dial scrubbed of any KMLE asset.
- **No on-camera dialogue from Jason as protagonist.** Per `feedback_sc_no_on_camera_dialogue.md` and `Style_Sheets/Elevated_Documentary.md` §0.2. SC ads' AI subjects do not speak on camera. The existing UGC variant (`Jason/05` §4) is the only carve-out and it is not in this set.
- **No crash footage. No hospital scenes. No surgery aestheticization.** SC brand-system standard.
- **Character lock per `Jason/05` §3** in any frame featuring Jason's body or hands: walker/cane on the LEFT, calloused right palm, wedding band on left ring finger, 5-day stubble, Carhartt overshirt unbuttoned, slip-on Crocs (Weeks 1–5) or unlaced Red Wing Iron Rangers (Weeks 6+). Concepts that don't feature Jason's body sidestep this.
- **Voice test 3/3 per `Jason/00` §7.** All five concepts cleared 3/3 in Ferrier's audit. Re-verify before final ship.
- **Caption discipline per `feedback_ad_post_workflow.md`.** CapCut auto-captions, Inter Bold white with 2px black stroke, bottom-center 250px safe zone. Required on every video; concept 05 is sound-mandatory and the captions are load-bearing.
- **Branding in last pass per `feedback_branding_last_pass.md`.** Fair Case torch + Torch Amber CTA chip + WCTL end-card go in CapCut, NOT baked into Higgsfield stills/videos.
- **Overlay palette per `feedback_fair_case_overlay_palette_rule.md`.** Hook cards / kinetic text use SC parent palette (Cream / Burgundy / Warm Charcoal). Roman Navy + Torch Amber appear at the CTA chip + form only.

## Voice Test (the kill criteria — applied to all 5 finalists)

Three questions from `Jason/00_Executive_Summary.md` §7. **Each finalist scored 3/3 in Ferrier's audit.** Re-verify before any concept enters production.

1. *Would Jason read this and feel **recognized**, not pitied?*
2. *Would Jason **screenshot this and send it to his crew text thread or his cousin Pete with no caption**?*
3. *Does it name the role inversion **without naming it directly**?*

3/3 ships. 2/3 revises. 1/3 dies.

## How These Concepts Get Built

Per concept file (`01..05`):

1. McCabe's hook is locked → Higgsfield reference-image flow per `feedback_higgsfield_workflow.md` (ref image carries character, prompts describe action not character).
2. Per-concept Higgsfield batch: 8–12 still generations to lock the visual register, best frame forwards as next ref.
3. Kling 2.6 motion (where applicable) — prompts compressed under 2500-char limit per `feedback_kling_26_prompt_char_limit.md`. Motivated lively camera movement (default slow patient dolly+tilt) per `feedback_motion_prompt_defaults.md`.
4. ElevenLabs TTS for any VO — Bill voice primary per `project_sc_vo_is_elevenlabs.md`. Concept 05 needs a second TTS voice cast separately.
5. CapCut final pass: captions + overlays + Fair Case torch + WCTL end-card.
6. Pre-ship: Saul gates compliance per concept's flag list. Robert reviews substantiated claims (the dollar amounts in Concepts 03 and 04, the procedural claims in Concepts 01 and 02).

## Phase 2 Read-Forward

If the bold sister-line outperforms the Cinematic line on the male audience by ≥20% on CTR or quiz-completion-rate over 14 days, **the male volume strategy shifts from "Cinematic primary, sister as variant" to "Cinematic + sister equal-weight."** Phase 2 picks up:

- **Trade-belt male personas across non-concrete trades** (HVAC, electrical, plumbing) — the Trade Tutorial register (Concept 02) is the canonical extender.
- **Workers'-comp differentiator persona** if Concept 02's workers'-comp Step 3 over-converts — this becomes its own creative line.
- **PS2-cutscene format extended to other personas** (Sarah's trucking case, Ty's motorcycle case) if Concept 03 works — the format is genre-agnostic.

If the sister-line *under*-performs, the campaign reverts to Cinematic-only for the male slot and we re-tune the trade specificity (HVAC instead of concrete) before retiring the bold register entirely.
