## ⚠ Production reality (S1 primary actual cut)

Original brief specified a clean dusk wide of I-17 with a Freightliner in distance going same direction (no crash). **Final production diverged**: the primary opens with an explicit AZ-desert crash aftermath (white trailer truck #481361 on roadside, smoke flare, motorcycle helmet, debris, broken-down sedan), then escalates through corporate insurance imagery and the WCTL "$1.7 BILLION RECOVERED" wall, ending on the Fair Case quiz form. **AI crash aftermath imagery is in-bounds for AZ pilot** (no SB-37) and the craft bar is met (golden-hour photographic, no impact-shock, no injured bodies).

The **Anchor cohort was dropped** from the actual production — captions float over footage instead of sitting in a Fair Case-branded bottom panel. Variants below are restructured to the actual production arc — **Aftermath → System Imbalance → Counter-Force → Funnel CTA** — so they slot into the same 5-visual production pattern Davis proved on the primary.

## Why trucking, why Sarah

Trucking = **highest-case-value vertical**. Commercial carriers carry $1M+ policies; compound humerus from a Freightliner rear-end is a policy-limits case. Smallest pool (~91 incidents/month AZ); hook hyperspecifically self-qualifies.

Sarah is the archetype: ICU Charge Nurse at AZ's only Level I trauma center north of Phoenix. Knows commercial insurance from the hospital side. Reachable through *clinical competence*, not "fight for your rights" energy.

> *"I know what my ER bill was — and it was forty-seven."*
> *"Truck accidents are not car accidents. The lawyer should be different."*
> *"Commercial carriers have legal teams on call 24/7. You should too."*

### Visual direction
**Cinematic motion** — slow camera moves, atmospheric. Each clip is a held composition with subtle drift. Golden-hour exterior + warm interior lighting. Per Davis production rule: **branding lives only on the Visual 5 quiz form** (Fair Case logo + WCTL sponsorship disclaimer appear natively as part of the funnel screen) — no torch baked into clips 1–4.

### Rationale
**Specificity + scale + counter-force + funnel.** The aftermath specifically anchors AZ + I-17 + Freightliner (filters non-trucking, non-AZ audiences in five words). The insurance tower visualizes the *scale* of the opposing force most viewers underestimate. The multi-floor offices show *they're already working*. The WCTL conference room with the $1.7B plaque is the **counter-force reveal** — the firm at the same scale of competence. Funnel quiz delivers the action.
