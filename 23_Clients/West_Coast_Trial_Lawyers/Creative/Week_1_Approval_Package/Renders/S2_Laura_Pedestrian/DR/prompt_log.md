## Iteration targets (apply to every Higgsfield render)

1. **Side-specific continuity props (load-bearing — match S2 reference_lock):**
   - **Cane in LEFT hand** (medical-issue cane, simple aluminum or hardwood, NOT a fashion accessory). NEVER right.
   - **ACE wrap subtly visible at RIGHT ankle** below the jean cuff — beige compression wrap, not bandage-white.
   - **Pale cream-grey cardigan** over a white t-shirt. NOT colorful, NOT athletic-wear. Quiet librarian / neighborhood register.
   - **Simple slip-on Sperrys or canvas flats**, not sneakers.
   - **Gilbert & Warner intersection** as the canonical setting — palo-verde tree visible in deep background where it was on the day, same crosswalk paint, same pole-mounted "WALK" sign post.
   - **Three-quarter body angle camera-left**, looking down at the painted crosswalk lines (NOT at camera).

2. **Light register:** **dusk only.** Cyan-fade upper sky, amber streetlight upper-right casting warm key on her face from frame-right, warm-sand asphalt foreground. NEVER midday harsh sun, NEVER night-black.

3. **Type legibility on dark-gradient:** minimum 70% black-to-warm-charcoal gradient over photographic background where serif copy lands. Falls off into full-color photo bleed in lower 30-40%.

4. **No text in the rendered Higgsfield image** for V1, V2, V5, V6 (CapCut text overlay). V3 + V4 use ChatGPT Image 2 with editorial text rendering — verify spelling against locked Copy Treatment.

5. **No AI tells:** waxy skin, symmetrical face, HDR-glow, plastic eyes, melted hands, billboard energy.

6. **Branding-last-pass:** Fair Case torch (white, small, bottom-right), Torch Amber typography on closing line, IBM Plex Mono 8pt footer — all CapCut. No URL in-image.

7. **Reference image carry:** start each generation with the locked Laura reference portrait file as the character anchor. Prompts describe ACTION + ENVIRONMENT, never re-describe Laura's face / build / age.

```
[REFERENCE IMAGE: Laura reference_lock loaded.]

Cinematic photographic still. The woman in the picture — Laura, early-40s
librarian — stands at the near-side curb of the Gilbert & Warner crosswalk,
three-quarter body angle camera-left, looking down at the painted white
crosswalk lines (NOT at camera). Cane in her LEFT hand. ACE wrap subtle at
her RIGHT ankle below jean cuff. Pale cream-grey cardigan over a white

horizon line). Amber streetlight upper-right casting warm key on her face

visible in deep OOF background camera-left. The painted crosswalk lines
lead diagonally into the frame.

Composition: upper 60% of frame is dusk sky + the warm-charcoal scrim zone
that will receive headline type in CapCut. Lower 40% is the photo focus —
Laura, curb, crosswalk lines.

4:5 portrait. Mid-format film register: Mamiya 645 with 80mm equivalent,
ISO 400, natural light only. Soth + Kander quiet observational palette. NO
HDR. Place-as-protagonist composition.

NO text in the image. NO logos. NO graphic overlays.

Avoid: harsh light, billboard energy, AI tells (waxy skin, symmetrical face,
melted hands), bright daylight, midday register.
```
