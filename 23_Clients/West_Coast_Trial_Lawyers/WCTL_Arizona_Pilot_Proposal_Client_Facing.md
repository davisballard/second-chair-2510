## How We Qualify Leads

Every lead that reaches your team has passed through this:

1. **Accident verification** — Were they in an accident? When? (We prioritize leads within 18 months to ensure your team has adequate time to evaluate and file)
2. **Fault filter** — Not at fault or shared fault where they're not primary
3. **Accident type** — MVA, trucking, motorcycle, pedestrian, cyclist, rideshare, DUI victim
4. **Injury severity** — This is the critical step. They must indicate non-soft-tissue injuries: broken bones, surgery, hospitalization, herniated disc, TBI, concussion, lacerations, burns, or comparable. Soft tissue (whiplash only, minor bruising, neck/back pain only) gets filtered out here.
5. **No existing attorney** — Already represented = disqualified
6. **Contact verification** — Valid phone + email confirmed
7. **TCPA consent** — 1-to-1 consent that explicitly names West Coast Trial Lawyers. The FCC's 1-to-1 consent requirement was struck down, but we still implement it as best practice — it's a higher standard that protects both sides.
8. **Bot/fraud filter** — Automated detection for fake submissions
