## Key exchanges (curated)

**Julian (the reframe move):**
> *"This isn't an arm-for-the-boss call anymore. He picked us — the work needs to look smart so he looks smart. Walk him through the craft. What formats, why each, how a user gets hit. The agenda becomes: personas quick-recap, format catalog deep, journey deep, phase reframe, answer-anything window."*

**Hegarty on the format-job split:**
> *"Cinematic primaries do recognition. Cinematic stills do contemplation. UGC does pattern break. Motion-stills bridge static into Reels. DR print stills convert. Designed-statics signal authority. One format can't do all six jobs — that's why six formats exist. Single-format campaigns are leaving half of Meta's surfaces empty."*

**Indigo on placement-format-mood matching:**
> *"Reels 9:16 lives where she's actively scrolling on her phone in the morning or at night. Feed 1:1 lives in lunch-break passive scroll. Stories lives in quick swipe pace. Each format is built to its placement — and each placement is a different mood-state. Multi-format = multi-mood coverage. That's how you get 9 effective touches inside Meta's 7-per-week frequency cap."*

**Massimo on the designed-static brand units:**
> *"HN-1.7B and EX-Settlement-Gap aren't trying to do recognition or conversion. They do brand-trust authority. The user who's been hit by 3-4 persona ads needs to know they're talking to a real firm before they'll convert. Restricted color, IBM Plex Mono, grid-disciplined. Restraint signals authority more than abundance does."*

**McCabe on the DR print stills:**
> *"Editorial register, not billboard. Layout-first per the Copy Treatment discipline. Headline does most of the work — *'Hit by a Freightliner on I-17?'*, *'Hit while crossing the street?'*, *'A driver didn't see the rider.'*, *'Broken bones. Bills don't stop.'* No late-night-TV-lawyer energy. The hook is the filter; the layout is the authority signal."*

**Roma (still on standby for objections):**
> *"He won't push back like a prospect — but his team will when he walks this back. Anticipate: 'why so many formats,' 'why static-only for Laura/M3,' 'why UGC for Diana but not the others,' 'why no crash footage,' 'how is Phase 2 cheaper.' Pre-answer all of those in the cheatsheet's answer-anything section."*
