### On the parasocial implication for Phase 2

**Shotton (pulled in):**
> "The reason talking-head wins in PI specifically isn't just 'people trust faces.' It's *parasocial trust transfer* — viewers form weak bonds with on-camera personas they see repeatedly. Morgan's John Morgan running 30-second ads daily for years builds parasocial brand equity that converts on a single ad view. Cinematic-doc creative doesn't do that — there's no recurring face. Implication: if Phase 2 pivots toward talking-head, *commit to a recurring persona.* Don't rotate AI characters. Pick one and let parasocial equity compound."

**Hegarty:**
> "Cinematic creative is for *campaign* lift; talking-head is for *brand* lift. Phase 1 tests both; Phase 2 commits."
