### Differentiator
> *"No vendor reports per-lead value like this. They report CPL — what you paid for a phone number — or CPSC 6-12 months after cases close. Neither shows the VALUE of what's in the pipeline before it signs."*

> *"CPSC averages every signed case. Ten soft-tissue cases = same CPSC as nine soft-tissue + one trucking, even though revenue is completely different. Projected Attorney Fee reflects case-value mix because it scores each lead individually."*
