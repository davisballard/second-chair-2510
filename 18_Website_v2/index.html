The Compliance

FCC 2025 built-in. Not bolted on after the fact.

Before Jan 2025
1 Lead Generated
Firm A Firm B Firm C Firm D
Same lead sold four times. Blanket checkbox. No named consent.
After FCC 2025
1 Lead Generated
Your Firm Only ✓
One-to-one named consent. Your lead. Your claimant. Zero shared exposure.

The January 2025 FCC ruling changed what compliant lead generation looks like. One-to-one, explicitly named consent is now required before any robocall or automated text contact. The old blanket checkbox — the one most lead vendors still use — is no longer sufficient.

We built our entire consent architecture around this standard from day one. Every lead your firm receives was generated with named, one-to-one consent for your firm specifically. Your leads are clean. Your compliance exposure is zero.

TrustedForm certified. DNC-screened. Fail-closed by default. Every lead passes through compliance verification before it reaches your CRM. If a lead fails any check, it never gets delivered.
