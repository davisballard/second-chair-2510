## What Fair Case Is

Fair Case is the consumer-facing brand under Second Chair. It is what injured people see — never what law firms see. Law firms see Second Chair. Claimants see Fair Case.

The funnel at getafaircase.com qualifies personal injury leads through a 6-question quiz, collects TCPA-compliant consent, and routes verified leads to Second Chair's firm partners. Fair Case has no direct relationship with attorneys — it is purely the intake front door.
