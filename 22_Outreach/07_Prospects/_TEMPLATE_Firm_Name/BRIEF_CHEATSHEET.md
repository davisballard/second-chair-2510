## PHASE 2 — Creative Discovery

**Davis-declarative opening frame:**
> *"One thing that's central to how we work — showing clients the actual creative before it runs. Non-negotiable for us. Partly brand coherence, partly {{compliance}}. So I'm curious how things work on your side."*

**Q1:** *"Are your current lead vendors showing you the ad creative they're running for {{FIRM_NAME}}?"* *(silence 4+s)*

**Release valve:** *"Yeah — honestly, that's the industry norm. Nobody in your role gets shown the creative."*

**Q2 — denial close:** *"For all we know the creative is completely clean. Possible, right? So the real question isn't whether it's clean — it's how you'd know."*

## 10 Golden Rules

1. **Slow down.** Late-night DJ voice.
2. **70% questions / 30% statements.**
3. **Critique the industry, never their vendors.**
4. **Give them the insight, not yourself.**
5. **4+ seconds silence after each question.**
6. **Accusation audit at every transition.**
7. **Arm them for {{DM_NAME}} at every objection.**
8. **Precise numbers.** $5K-$100K, not "five figures."
9. **Fairness trigger for pricing.**
10. **Don't compete on risk.** Refuse guarantees with questions.
