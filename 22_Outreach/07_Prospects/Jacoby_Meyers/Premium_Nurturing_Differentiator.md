## Executive Summary

Jacoby & Meyers' 24/7 intake capability makes them the **perfect client for premium live transfer tier**. This positions Second Chair not just as a lead vendor, but as an integrated lead gen + intake partner — a capability Settlement Allies cannot match.

**Key insight:** J&M's CPSC guarantee request reveals they value conversion rate over upfront cost. Live transfers deliver exactly what they're asking for: dramatically higher conversion (40-60% vs. 10-15%) at predictable cost per signed case.

**Strategic recommendation:** Position premium tier as the solution to their CPSC concern, not a concession on the guarantee structure.

### Cost Per Signed Case Math: Premium vs. CPA

**Settlement Allies (CPA model):**
- Cost per signed case: $2,500-$6,500 (they set the price)
- Cash flow: Payment 30-60 days after case signs
- Risk: Zero (only pay for signed cases)
- Control: Vendor decides which leads to send (black box)

**Second Chair Premium Tier:**
- Cost per lead: $1,510
- Conversion rate: 50% (conservative estimate for 24/7 intake)
- **Cost per signed case: $1,510 ÷ 0.5 = $3,020**
- Cash flow: Weekly billing (much faster)
- Risk: Client takes conversion risk
- Control: Client gets all qualified leads (transparent)

**J&M perspective:**
- SC premium: $3,020/case (20% higher than SA's low end)
- But: Cash flow is 4-6 weeks faster
- And: Client controls which cases to sign (not vendor)
- And: Transparent reporting (not black box)

**The pitch:**
> "Settlement Allies charges $2,500-$6,500 per signed case and you wait 30-60 days for billing. Our premium tier delivers $3,020 per signed case with weekly billing. You're paying 20% more than SA's low end, but you're getting paid 4-6 weeks faster and you control which cases you sign. Plus, you get DMA exclusivity — SA doesn't offer that."

## Objection Handling: Premium Tier Specific

### Objection 1: "That's too expensive. $1,510 per lead?"

**Don't say:** "But it converts better..."

**Do say:**
> "I hear you — $1,510 feels like a lot compared to $310. Let me show you the math:
>
> **Standard tier:**
> - Cost: $310/lead
> - Conversion: 12% (you'll do better than average with 4-min response, but let's be conservative)
> - Cost per signed case: $310 ÷ 0.12 = $2,583
>
> **Premium tier:**
> - Cost: $1,510/lead
> - Conversion: 60% (you have 24/7 intake, so conversion will be at top of industry range)
> - Cost per signed case: $1,510 ÷ 0.60 = $2,517
>
> You're paying 5x more per lead but getting lower cost per signed case. And you need 83% fewer leads to hit the same case volume. Your intake team spends 75% less time on calls.
>
> The question isn't 'is $1,510 expensive?' — it's 'do we want to call 100 leads to get 12 cases, or qualify and transfer 30 leads to get 18 cases?' It's about efficiency, not just price."

---

### Objection 2: "Why not just use Settlement Allies?"

**Don't say:** "We're better than them..."

**Do say:**
> "Settlement Allies is a great option if you want zero conversion risk. They charge $2,500-$6,500 per signed case and you only pay when a case signs. The trade-offs are:
>
> 1. **Payment delay:** They bill 30-60 days after case signs. We bill weekly.
> 2. **Cost:** At $2,500-$6,500/case, you might pay more. Our premium tier at 60% conversion is $2,517/case — mid-range of their pricing.
> 3. **Control:** They decide which leads to send (black box). You get every qualified lead we generate (transparent).
> 4. **DMA exclusivity:** They don't offer it. We do — you're the only firm in Colorado.
>
> If you want lowest risk, Settlement Allies wins. If you want fastest cash flow, lowest cost per case, and territorial exclusivity, we win. It depends on which matters more to you."

---

### Objection 3: "Can we just do standard tier for now?"

**Don't say:** "But premium is better..."

**Do say:**
> "Absolutely. Most firms start with standard tier to test lead quality, then layer on premium tier once they see conversion rates. The premium tier isn't required — it's an optional upgrade.
>
> Here's what I'd suggest: Let's start with 100 standard leads. You'll see conversion rates, case values, and lead quality. If you like what you see and you want to improve conversion from 10-15% to 40-60%, we can add premium tier at that point.
>
> The only reason I'm bringing it up now is because you have 24/7 intake — the perfect setup for live transfers. Most firms don't have that advantage. But if you want to start with standard and prove it out first, that's totally fine. Your call."

**Why this works:**
- Removes pressure (their choice)
- Plants seed for future upgrade
- Emphasizes their unique advantage (24/7)
- Shows flexibility and confidence
