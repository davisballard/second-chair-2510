---

## Rules (Spam Avoidance)

- **Plain text only.** No HTML, no images, no logos, no formatting.
- **No links.** No URLs anywhere in the email — not even in the signature. URLs trigger spam filters on outreach domains.
- **No image signature.** Text only: Name, title, Second Chair, phone number.
- **75-125 words max.** Shorter = personal. Longer = marketing.
- **After first reply,** subsequent emails can include links to 2ndchair.net.

## What NOT to Include

- No links (until they reply)
- No pricing or lead volumes
- No consumer brand names (Fair Case, etc.)
- No cost-per-signed-case figures
- No "we're different" — the credentials say it
- No urgency tactics or fake scarcity
