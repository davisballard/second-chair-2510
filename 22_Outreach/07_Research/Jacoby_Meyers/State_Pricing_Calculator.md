## Discount Framework

**State lockdown discount (15%) applies ONLY to California** — the only state in J&M's footprint where they would be locking down all (or nearly all) DMAs and where SC gets real operational efficiency from a single compliance framework, shared creative system, and consolidated campaigns.

**Every other DMA: list price.** Buying scattered DMAs across different states is independent work for SC — different compliance, different creative, different campaigns. The value J&M gets across markets isn't a price discount — it's territory exclusivity, one national partner, consolidated reporting, and priority creative.

All prices rounded to nearest $5.

## Presentation Strategy

**Davis leads pricing conversations. Alex handles product/platform questions.**

1. **Do NOT lead with discounts.** Present each market at its price. The price reflects the cost and value of that market.

2. **If J&M wants all of California:** *"When you lock down an entire state like California — all seven DMAs — we apply a 15% discount. There's real operational efficiency for us when we build one compliance framework, one creative system for the whole state. That's a meaningful commitment from both sides."*

3. **If they ask for discounts on other markets:** *"New York City is a $460 market. That reflects the cost of competing for attention in the most expensive PI market in the country — 5,000+ firms, $300-$500 CPCs, the strictest no-fault screening in the US. The price is the price because the cost is the cost."*

4. **What they get instead of a price discount:** *"What you get across all your markets is something no other vendor offers: territory exclusivity in every DMA, one national creative team, one dashboard, one relationship. That's the value of consolidating with us."*

5. **Always anchor on cost per signed case:**
   - At $383/lead, 10% sign rate → **~$3,830 cost per signed case**
   - Industry average from lead vendors: $8,000–$14,000
   - TV/Radio: $10,000–$16,000
   - Google Search: $12,000–$20,000
