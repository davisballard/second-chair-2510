| **Jacoby & Meyers Injury Lawyers** | jacobyandmeyers.com | California only | 14 CA offices |

The jacobymeyers.com contact page explicitly states: *"Jacoby & Meyers is comprised of independent law offices and attorneys throughout the United States."*

**What this means for the call:** Tommy's title is Head of Marketing — but for which entity? The California operation or the national brand? This determines whether we're pitching a 7-DMA California deal or a 17-DMA multi-state enterprise deal. **This must be surfaced on the call.**

## Key Intelligence for the Call

### What to Reference (builds rapport without flattery)

1. **"54 years in operation, offices coast to coast"** — Shows you've done your homework on the full operation, not just the California site.

2. **"Pioneered legal advertising on TV"** — If it comes up naturally: *"You changed how law firms reach clients in 1977. We're building the next version of that for digital."*

3. **"$2B+ recovered"** — Acknowledge THEIR credibility: *"A firm with your results should have a lead gen partner that reflects that quality."*

4. **"13 offices across the New York metro alone"** — This signals you understand their real scale.

### What NOT to Reference on the Call

- Broken attorney profile pages / off-site redirects
- The Dolman Law Group connection
- Unverifiable award badges
- The "independent law offices" network structure (save for contract stage)

### The First Question to Answer on the Call

> *"Tommy, you have a significant presence — offices in New York, California, Florida, DC, Pennsylvania, and beyond. When you think about lead gen, are you looking for coverage across all of those markets, or are there specific states you want to focus on first?"*

This question does three things:
1. Shows you know their full footprint (not just CA)
2. Lets Tommy tell you the actual scope of the deal
3. Avoids assuming the 4-state framing from the Lead Faucet era

## Competitive Context

**What J&M is probably buying now:**

At ~$100M+ revenue with 43 offices and 113 employees, J&M is likely spending significantly on lead gen:
- Multiple lead vendors across their geographic markets
- Local SEO/PPC agencies per region
- Possibly their own ganatucaso.com campaigns for Hispanic market

**SC's differentiation for this prospect:**

| What They're Used To | What SC Offers |
|---------------------|----------------|
| Shared leads (multiple firms get same lead) | Territory exclusivity — one firm per DMA |
| Generic national creative | DMA-specific advertising built for each market |
| Lead volume as the metric | Cost per signed case as the metric |
| Compliance as an afterthought | FCC 2025 compliant from day one |
| Black box performance | Every ad reviewable, live dashboard |
| Separate vendors per region | One partner covering all their DMAs nationally |

That last row is key: **SC can be J&M's single national lead gen partner** covering all 17 DMAs under one contract. No other vendor offers territory exclusivity across that many markets.
